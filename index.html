About

Flesh & Code is a hybrid philosophical–artistic project: 320 aphorisms (“arrows”) inspired by phenomenology and glitch aesthetics become images, loops, audio and objects. It proposes a new ontology where bodies and technologies interpenetrate.

Your support brings this constellation to life—from Guadalajara’s premiere to international screens. Funds are used to produce visuals and sound, craft tactile sculptures, and sustain the archive. Join us in shaping the digital flesh.
